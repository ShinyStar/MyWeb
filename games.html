The Void

When you feel yourself hollow, when all your hard work changes nothing, when you feel alone in darkness, trapped within your own mind, will you still seek for happiness? Will you dare keep fighting to escape the Void?
It is a dungeon crawler game. The player must go through endless procedurally generated levels defeating enemies with the objective of getting as far as possible, building a score as high as possible.

This game won the Imagine Cup 2016 Online Finals and World Semifinals

Not A Cat!

Not A Cat is a game being developed by Binary Tales in which you control Silk, the little ruler of Karistalia. Silk is a Quartz Basilisk, a rare species that is born each time the last one passes away and whose fate is to rule Karistalia. This fact has been forgotten by Karistalians for long now and they are confused by Silk's looks. Since the first time Silk was told it looked like a cat, everyone else thinks so too which makes him really uncomfortable. However, what really made him explode was his friend Kil calling him that too. Now Silk must prove the world he's not a cat while fixing everything he messes up in his rage along the way.
It is a platformer game in which you must use the powers of the elements to make your way through neverending dungeons.

This game won the Imagine Cup 2014 Online Finals
